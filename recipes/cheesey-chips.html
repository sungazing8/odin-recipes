Cheesey Chips

Get your calories for the week in, with these lovely fattening Cheesey Chips!

prep time: 10 mins

cook time: 15 mins

Serves one. get em down ya fatty!.

Ingredients:

  • 2-3 medium sized baking potatoes
  • 1 chip pan filled with oil (prefreably over a decade old for best results)
  • half a block of your favourite cheese
  • handful of basil leaves (optional)
  • 2 slices of bread
  • knob of butter

    Cooking method

  1. Set the chip pan on the stove on at a medium heat setting, start a timer for 10 minutes
  2. Fill the sink with water. peel and chip the potatoes. place the chipped potatoes on a piece of kitchen roll to soak up the excess water
  3. Once the 10 minute timer is up, put the cut chips into the pan. set another 10 minute timer
  4. while the chips are cooking use a bowl and grater to grate the cheese to the desired amount. put fucking loads in. you know you want to.
  5. Turn the chips over periodically until the timer runs out.
  6. At the 10 minute mark, reset the timer for another 5 minutes, then butter the tow slices of bread
  7. Once the 5 minute mark is up, drain the chips of excess oil and place on a plate. Add the cheese and then nom down.
